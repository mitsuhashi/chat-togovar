The single nucleotide polymorphism (SNP) **rs794727152** is associated with a pathogenic variant in the *CFTR* gene, specifically the c.1521_1523del (p.Phe508del) mutation, which is commonly known as the F508del mutation. This variant is a well-known cause of cystic fibrosis (CF). 

The **minor allele frequency (MAF)** of rs794727152 is very low in the general population because it is a pathogenic variant. According to population databases such as gnomAD (Genome Aggregation Database), the MAF varies depending on the population but is generally less than **1%** in most groups. For example, it is more common in individuals of European ancestry, where it can reach a frequency of approximately **0.02–0.03 (2–3%)** in heterozygous carriers.

To get the most accurate and up-to-date MAF for rs794727152, you can refer to databases like [gnomAD](https://gnomad.broadinstitute.org/) or [dbSNP](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/snp/).
