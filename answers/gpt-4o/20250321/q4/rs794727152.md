The variant **rs794727152** is located in the **CFTR** gene and is associated with cystic fibrosis. It is a single nucleotide variant (SNV) where the **reference allele** is **C** (cytosine), and the **alternative allele** is **A** (adenine). This variant is also known as **c.1652G>A** in the CFTR gene, which corresponds to the amino acid change **p.Gly551Asp (G551D)**.
