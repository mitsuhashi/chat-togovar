### English:
The variant **rs727504136** is classified as a **single nucleotide variant (SNV)**. It is located in the **BRCA1 gene**, which is associated with hereditary breast and ovarian cancer syndrome. Below is detailed information about this variant:

---

#### 1. **Variant Identification**:
- **rs number**: rs727504136  
- **HGVS notation**: NM_007294.3:c.5074G>A  
- **Gene name**: BRCA1  
- **Transcript name**: NM_007294.3  
- **Location**: Chromosome 17 (GRCh38: chr17:41276045)  
- **Links**: [dbSNP rs727504136](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/snp/rs727504136)

---

#### 2. **Disease Associations**:
This variant has been reported in **ClinVar** as pathogenic and is associated with **hereditary breast and ovarian cancer syndrome**. It results in a missense change (p.Arg1692Gln), which affects the BRCA1 protein's function. Predictive models such as **SIFT** and **PolyPhen** classify this variant as damaging.

---

#### 3. **Literature Evidence**:
This variant has been mentioned in studies investigating BRCA1 mutations in individuals with familial breast and ovarian cancer. For example, it is discussed in research on BRCA1/BRCA2 mutation carriers in various populations. Specific publications can be found in PubMed, such as [PMID: 29446198](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/29446198/).

---

#### 4. **Allele Frequency Comparison**:
The allele frequency of rs727504136 is extremely low in global populations, including Japanese and non-Japanese groups. According to gnomAD, it is classified as a rare variant, with no significant population-specific differences reported.

---

#### 5. **GWAS Findings**:
No GWAS studies have directly linked rs727504136 to phenotypes beyond its known association with hereditary breast and ovarian cancer.
