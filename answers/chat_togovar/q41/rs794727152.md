### Summary of Similar Effects
Both variants (G>A and G>C) at the same genomic position (chr2:165342465) in the **SCN2A** gene result in amino acid changes at position 853 of the protein:
- **G>A**: Arginine (Arg) to Glutamine (Gln)
- **G>C**: Arginine (Arg) to Proline (Pro)

These changes are predicted to have severe effects on protein function, as indicated by predictive models (SIFT, PolyPhen-2, AlphaMissense). Both variants are associated with **Developmental and Epileptic Encephalopathy, 11 (DEE11)** and other neurodevelopmental disorders.
